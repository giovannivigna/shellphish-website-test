Shellphish at DEF CON

Shellphish has participated to more DEF CON Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions than any other team in history, winning the competition in 2005.

In addition, many of the Shellphish team members participated in the organization of several editions of the DEF CON CTF as part of different teams, namely as members of the Order of the Overflow (OOO) and Nautilus.
