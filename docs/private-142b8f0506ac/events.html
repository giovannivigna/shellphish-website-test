Shellphish Memorable Events

The Shellphish team has organized or participated in many CTFs and other challenges over the years. Here are some of the most memorable ones. (P.S. if you are a Shellphish member and you have additional pictures, videos, or stories about these events, please contact team@#shellphish.net.)

    The 2005 DEF CON CTF was the first competition in which Shellphish participated. The competiion was organized by kenshoto (and Shellphish won the competition.)
